Blue Lock



Synopsis

Blue lock is an anime made by Muneyuki Kaneshiro, the main theme of Blue Lock focuses on Egoism and an individualistic type of sports play which makes this anime very unique. It features 300 players; however, only 1 player can be the best striker. Th2 remaining 299 players that failed to prove themselves will no longer be able to play football at an international level.

Yoichi Isagi





Is the protagonist of the Blue Lock Series, he is an underdog who possess a very high type of intelligence when it comes to football. He is able to adapt to the flow of the battle when it comes to football. He is voiced by Fajardo, Ricco as the English VA , and Ura Kazuki as the Japanese Voice actor
